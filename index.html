Car Fuel Efficiency in 2017

Mohini Purandare CS416 Narrative Visualization Project 07/28/2024

This Martini Glass Visualization compares the fuel efficiency in miles per gallon (MPG) for Electric and Gasoline cars for various car makers in 2017. Various factors that influence a car's fuel efficiency are examined like whether the vehicle is driven in the city or on the highway. The aim to provide insight into what kind of cars provide the best mileage.

At the end of the visualization, you can explore the details more by interacting with the graphs.
